Metronomics: The Science Behind the Learning

A Professional Development course that will help you understand the proven benefits of using songs, lyrics and music videos to boost achievement and develop the whole child.

Created to help teachers embed core Literacy skills across the wider curriculum, this course focuses on the fascinating research of Metronomics: the science of how music enables the brain to acquire language both quickly and securely. 

It will also look at the wider benefits of using song lyrics in the classroom and explain the best ways in which to use music videos effectively to maximise their impact.
